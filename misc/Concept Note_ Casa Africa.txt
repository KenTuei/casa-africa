Concept Note: Casa Africa 
1. Background & Rationale 
Africa faces persistent structural challenges in healthcare delivery, financial inclusion, workforce 
management, retail, mobility, and access to reliable digital services. While technology adoption 
is accelerating, many solutions remain fragmented, expensive, or poorly adapted to African 
contexts. There is a grow ing need for locally built, scalable software products that directly 
address African market realities while meeting global standards. 
Casa Africa is established as a software venture focused on building and commercializing 
practical, high-impact digital products tailored for African institutions, businesses, and 
consumers. The company w ill develop, package, and sell subscription-based and enterprise 
software solutions across multiple sectors, prioritizing usability, affordability, and scalability. 
2. Purpose of Casa Africa 
The purpose of Casa Africa is to design, build, and sell software products that solve real 
operational and service-delivery challenges in Africa. Casa Africa aims to bridge the gap 
between innovation and adoption by delivering production-ready software that institutions and 
businesses can deploy immediately. 
3. Vision and Mission 
Vision: 
To become a leading African software house delivering trusted, scalable digital solutions that 
power institutions, businesses, and everyday life across the continent. 
Mission: 
To build and commercialize practical software products that improve efficiency, transparency, 
access, and user experience across critical African sectors. 
4. Core Software Products 
Casa Africa’s initial product portfolio includes the follow ing solutions: 
4.1 Immuno 
A digital health and immunization management platform designed to support health facilities, 
NGOs, and governments in tracking immunization coverage, facility performance, and child 
health outcomes. The system enables data-driven decision-making and improved service 
delivery. 
 
4.2 AI Disprover (Browser Extension) 
A lightweight browser extension that detects, flags, and verifies AI-generated or manipulated 
content. The tool supports content authenticity, academ ic integrity, and trust in digital 
information, particularly for schools, institutions, and organizations. 
4.3 Seam lessHR (HR System) 
A workforce and human resource management system inspired by existing HR platforms, 
adapted for African SMEs and institutions. Features include employee records, payroll 
integration readiness, leave management, performance tracking, and compliance support. 
4.4 Debt Collection System  
A digital debt management and collection platform for SACCOs, lenders, and financial 
institutions. The system supports borrower tracking, automated rem inders, reporting, and 
compliance workflows to improve recovery rates and operational efficiency. 
4.5 Robes – Smart Fashion Commerce Platform  
An e-commerce fashion platform that allows customers to shop using their exact body size. 
Robes leverages 3D modelling and customer photos to determ ine accurate sizing, reducing 
returns and improving the shopping experience for apparel buyers. 
4.6 Trot – Smart Car Rental Platform  
A car rental management and customer-facing platform enhanced w ith image recognition. Trot 
supports vehicle condition checks, damage detection, booking management, and fraud 
reduction for car rental companies. 
4.7 Blockchain Cross-Branch Banking MVP 
A blockchain-based m inimum viable product (MVP) designed to support secure cross-branch 
banking operations. The system enables branch staff to open new customer accounts, reset 
PINs, replace cards, and update Know Your Customer (KYC) information while maintaining an 
immutable audit trail of all actions. Built on a perm issioned blockchain network, the MVP 
ensures that updates made at one branch are securely synchronized across multiple branches 
in real time. The solution includes a simple, role-based front-end interface for branch staff and 
simulates real-world branch operations to demonstrate transparency, security, and 
interoperability across a distributed banking environment. 
4.8 24-Hour AI Chatbot 
An AI-powered, always-on chatbot that enables businesses to respond to customer inquiries 
24/7 across websites, WhatsApp, and social platforms. The chatbot handles FAQs, lead 
qualification, bookings, and customer support automation.
